mau5trap

mau5trap (pronounced "mousetrap") is a Canadian independent record label founded in 2007 by electronic music producer deadmau5. In June 2013, mau5trap partnered with Astralwerks, a subsidiary of Universal Music Group. Presently, as of October 2015, mau5trap has gone independent with music-rights and publishing at Kobalt.

Since its creation, the label has acquired a wide variety of talented artists, such as REZZ and Skrillex.
